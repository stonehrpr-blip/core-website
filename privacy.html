Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026 · Last updated: June 18, 2026

This is a starting template — have a privacy professional or lawyer review it for your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, Apple/Google requirements) before you publish.

This Privacy Policy explains how Core Studio (“CORE,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the CORE mobile app and website (the “Service”). By using the Service, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — email address and sign-in details when you create an account.
  • Usage data — habits you track, slips you log, streaks, scores, and in-app activity.
  • Content you provide — proof photos, reflections, and messages you send to the AI coach.
  • Device & technical data — device type, OS, app version, and basic analytics.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and personalize the Service, including your Life Score and AI coaching.
  • To process subscriptions and payments (via our payment providers).
  • To improve the app, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To communicate with you about your account and updates.

3. AI processing

To power the AI coach and verification features, some content (such as text or proof photos) may be processed by third-party AI providers. We only share what is needed to deliver the feature.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with service providers who help us operate the Service (e.g., hosting, payments, analytics, AI), under contracts that protect your data, or where required by law.

5. Your choices & rights

  • Social features are opt-in; sensitive habits are private by default.
  • You can access, export, or delete your data from in-app settings or by contacting us.
  • Depending on your region, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA.

6. Data retention & security

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, and we use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it.

7. Children

CORE is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll post the new effective date here and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at corestudiosupport@gmail.com.
